How do I disable cookies?

You may block cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse accepting cookies. However, if you select this setting, you will have a negative impact on the user experience of our website. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, Quickspin will issue cookies when you access our Website.

If you object to having cookies placed on your machine, you need to disable them. To do so, please follow the relevant instructions depending on your browser:

  • If you use the Internet Explorer browser.
  • If you use Firefox browser.
  • If you use the Safari browser
  • If you use the Google Chrome browser.
